### Pipeline Search and Synthesis Phase

#### Grammar Creation
- Task identified as SEMISUPERVISED_TASK.
- Grammar start state allows for pipelines composed of:
  - IMPUTER (sklearn.impute.SimpleImputer)
  - ENCODERS (optionally empty)
  - FEATURE_SCALER (various scalers or empty)
  - SEMISUPERVISED_CLASSIFIER (including AutonBox, SelfTrainingClassifier)
  - CLASSIFIER (multiple sklearn classifiers or empty)
  - LABELPROPAGATION_CLASSIFIER (LabelSpreading or LabelPropagation)

#### Iteration 1: Pipeline Search using MCTS (Monte Carlo Tree Search)
- Multiple MCTS simulations performed to expand possible pipeline structures.
- Initial pipeline S expanded into multiple variants combining:
  - IMPUTER
  - ENCODERS (mostly resolved to empty)
  - FEATURE_SCALER (tested as empty and various scalers like MaxAbsScaler, RobustScaler, StandardScaler)
  - SEMISUPERVISED_CLASSIFIER or LABELPROPAGATION_CLASSIFIER primitives.
- ENCODERS step largely removed (set to empty).
- IMPUTER consistently set to sklearn.impute.SimpleImputer.
- SEMISUPERVISED_CLASSIFIER or LABELPROPAGATION_CLASSIFIER primitives replaced by specific implementations such as:
  - SkLabelSpreading
  - SkLabelPropagation
  - AutonBox with base estimators (MultinomialNB or RandomForestClassifier)
  - SelfTrainingClassifier
- CLASSIFIER element appended with various sklearn classifiers, trials included:
  - RandomForestClassifier
  - MultinomialNB
  - KNeighborsClassifier
  - LinearDiscriminantAnalysis
- Frequent exploration of feature scalers; alternated between usage and omission.

#### Pipeline Construction Examples
- Pipeline with SimpleImputer, StandardScaler, SkLabelSpreading achieved score ~0.222.
- Pipeline with SimpleImputer, RobustScaler, SkLabelSpreading scored ~0.214.
- Pipeline with SimpleImputer, SkLabelSpreading (no scaler or encoder) achieved higher score of ~0.7588.
- Pipeline with SimpleImputer, RobustScaler, AutonBox with RandomForestClassifier scored ~0.7682 (highest score).
- Other tested pipelines included combinations of different semisupervised classifiers, feature scalers, and classifiers, with varying scores mostly below 0.3 except the two above.

### Finalization
- Search process stopped upon reaching timeout.
- Two pipelines identified as the best candidates:
  1. SimpleImputer + SkLabelSpreading (no scaler) with score ~0.7588
  2. SimpleImputer + RobustScaler + AutonBox with RandomForestClassifier with score ~0.7682
- These pipelines were recorded as outputs from the AutoML process.
