- Early scoring attempts of pipelines involving SkLabelSpreading encountered an AttributeError due to a DataFrame missing a 'toarray' method; this likely caused scoring failure for that pipeline.
- SelfTrainingClassifier pipelines using LinearSVC failed during scoring because LinearSVC lacks a 'predict_proba' method required by SelfTrainingClassifier.
- Various pipelines were evaluated, producing scores mostly around 0.15 to 0.24.
- A significant pipeline combining RobustScaler with SkLabelSpreading yielded a score of approximately 0.24.
- Later, a pipeline involving RobustScaler and AutonBox with SVC base estimator achieved a high score of about 0.6865.
