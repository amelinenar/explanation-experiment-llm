### Pipeline Search and Synthesis Phase

- A manual grammar with 44 productions was created for a TIME_SERIES_FORECAST_TASK.
- The top-level pipeline structure allowed two main options:
  1. Pipeline with IMPUTER, ENCODERS, FEATURE_SCALER, FEATURE_SELECTOR, and REGRESSOR.
  2. Pipeline with IMPUTER and TIME_SERIES_FORECAST module only.
- Encoders included various datetime encoding strategies (OrdinalEncoder, CyclicalFeature, Datetime64ExpandEncoder, DummyEncoder).
- Feature scalers included MaxAbsScaler, RobustScaler, StandardScaler, or none.
- Feature selectors included GenericUnivariateSelect, SelectPercentile, SelectKBest, or none.
- A wide range of regressors (linear models, trees, ensembles, support vector, Bayesian, XGBoost, LightGBM) was available.
- Time series forecasting modules included ArimaEstimator, DeeparEstimator, NBEATSEstimator, NHITSEstimator.

#### Iteration 1

- Multiple MCTS simulations were performed to explore pipeline options.
- Early simulations started from the initial state (S) and tried various decompositions.
- Decompositions considered either a full pipeline (IMPUTER+ENCODERS+FEATURE_SCALER+FEATURE_SELECTOR+REGRESSOR) or a simpler pipeline (IMPUTER+TIME_SERIES_FORECAST).
- Within the full pipeline branch, an encoder was moved to a DATETIME_ENCODER.
- The TIME_SERIES_FORECAST option was frequently tested, with the imputer moved to a null option ('E') in some cases.
- Specific time series forecasting models tested included NHITSEstimator, DeeparEstimator, ArimaEstimator, NBEATSEstimator.
- Predictions from the MCTS simulations ranged around 0.045 to 0.047, presumably indicating estimated performance metrics.
- The search converged on the pipeline: TIME_SERIES_FORECAST with NHITSEstimator (imputer removed).
