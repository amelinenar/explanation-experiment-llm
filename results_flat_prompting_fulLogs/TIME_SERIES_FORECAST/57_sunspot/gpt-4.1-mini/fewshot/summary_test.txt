### Pipeline Search and Synthesis Phase

#### Grammar Setup
- A manual grammar was created specific for the TIME_SERIES_FORECAST_TASK.
- The grammar consists of 44 production rules with start symbol S.
- Pipeline components allowed:
  - IMPUTER (allowed to be empty 'E')
  - ENCODERS with DATETIME_ENCODER options including sklearn's OrdinalEncoder and custom cyclic/datetime encoders.
  - FEATURE_SCALER including MaxAbsScaler, RobustScaler, StandardScaler, or none.
  - FEATURE_SELECTOR including various sklearn selectors or none.
  - REGRESSORS such as linear models, tree-based models, ensemble methods, and boosting libraries (XGBoost, LightGBM).
  - TIME_SERIES_FORECAST models including ArimaEstimator, DeeparEstimator, NBEATSEstimator, NHITSEstimator.

#### Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS) Pipeline Exploration
- **Iteration 1:**
  - Simulation 1 started from the root pipeline symbol S.
  - Prediction score recorded as approximately 0.0460.
  - Simulation 2 decomposed S into a multi-component pipeline: IMPUTER, ENCODERS, FEATURE_SCALER, FEATURE_SELECTOR, REGRESSOR.
    - Score around 0.0455.
  - Simulation 3 decomposed S to a simpler pipeline: IMPUTER, TIME_SERIES_FORECAST.
    - Score approx. 0.0462.
  - Simulation 4 expanded again into the multi-component pipeline.
    - Encoder selected: DATETIME_ENCODER (choosing ordinal encoding and others).
    - Score approx. 0.0460.
  - Simulation 5 returned to simple pipeline with TIME_SERIES_FORECAST after IMPUTER removal.
    - Score approx. 0.0469.
  
- The coach selects the TIME_SERIES_FORECAST pipeline (simpler pipeline without the encoding and scaling steps) for the next simulation round.

- Subsequent simulations explored various Time Series forecasting estimators in the pipeline:
  - NHITSEstimator
  - DeeparEstimator
  - ArimaEstimator
  - NBEATSEstimator

- Predictions for these models ranged tightly around 0.045 to 0.0469, indicating close performance and active exploration.

#### Final Pipeline Construction
- The finalized pipeline focused on TIME_SERIES_FORECAST model without an IMPUTER step (imputer removed as unnecessary).
- Selected estimator example: NHITSEstimator (chosen after exploration).
- This indicates the AutoML system prioritized specialized time series forecasting primitives over conventional preprocessing and regression pipelines for this task.
