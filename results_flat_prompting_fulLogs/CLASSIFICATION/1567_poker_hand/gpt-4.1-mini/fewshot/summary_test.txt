### Pipeline Search and Synthesis Phase

#### Grammar Setup
- A manual grammar for a classification task was created.
- Grammar includes pipeline components: IMPUTER, ENCODERS, FEATURE_SCALER, FEATURE_SELECTOR, CLASSIFIER.
- Various options are available for each component:
  - FEATURE_SCALER includes MaxAbsScaler, RobustScaler, StandardScaler, or none.
  - FEATURE_SELECTOR includes GenericUnivariateSelect, SelectPercentile, SelectKBest, or none.
  - CLASSIFIER includes a wide variety of classifiers such as LinearDiscriminantAnalysis, ExtraTreesClassifier, GradientBoostingClassifier, GaussianNB, KNeighborsClassifier, XGBClassifier, LGBMClassifier, and others.
- IMPUTER and ENCODERS default to no action ('E') since no missing values and no categorical encoding requirements were identified.

#### Iteration 1 - MCTS Simulations
- Multiple simulations of pipeline constructions and evaluations were performed via Monte-Carlo Tree Search (MCTS).
- Initial pipelines started from the start symbol `S`, expanded to include IMPUTER, ENCODERS, FEATURE_SCALER, FEATURE_SELECTOR, and CLASSIFIER components.
- Encoders and imputers were quickly set to no-ops ('E') due to data characteristics.
- FEATURE_SCALER consistently chosen as `sklearn.preprocessing.MaxAbsScaler`.
- FEATURE_SELECTOR consistently chosen as `sklearn.feature_selection.GenericUnivariateSelect`.
- Different classifiers were tested sequentially:
  - LinearDiscriminantAnalysis with a score ~0.479
  - ExtraTreesClassifier with a score ~0.457
  - GaussianNB with a score ~0.479
  - KNeighborsClassifier achieved a slightly better score ~0.481
  - GradientBoostingClassifier reached the best score ~0.500

#### Pipeline Construction and Scoring
- Pipelines constructed followed the pattern:
  1. Apply MaxAbsScaler to scale features.
  2. Apply GenericUnivariateSelect for feature selection.
  3. Apply a classifier (varied per simulation).
- Scoring was done after each pipeline instantiation.
- The best scoring pipeline used GradientBoostingClassifier with scaling and feature selection.

### Termination and Summary
- The search process timed out after approximately 7 minutes since the start of scoring.
- A total of 1 pipeline was retained/found after search termination.
- The best pipeline achieved a classification score (accuracy or other metric) of approximately 0.500.
