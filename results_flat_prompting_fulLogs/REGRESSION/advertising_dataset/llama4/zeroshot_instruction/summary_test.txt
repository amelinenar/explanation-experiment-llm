 ## Step 1: Initialization of the AutoML Process
The AutoML process begins with the initialization of the search for an optimal pipeline. This involves setting up the environment, defining the search space, and determining the criteria for evaluating pipelines.

## Step 2: Exploration of the Search Space
The system explores the search space by iteratively constructing pipelines. Each pipeline consists of a series of steps, including data preprocessing (e.g., scaling), feature selection, and regression modeling. The exploration involves creating and evaluating multiple pipelines based on predefined and dynamically generated steps.

## Step 3: Pipeline Construction and Evaluation
The AutoML system constructs pipelines by sequentially adding steps such as data scaling (e.g., using `StandardScaler` or `RobustScaler`), feature selection (e.g., using `GenericUnivariateSelect`), and regression modeling (e.g., using `LinearRegression`). Each pipeline is evaluated based on a scoring function, which assesses its performance.

## 4: Search Strategy and Heuristics
The search strategy involves using heuristics to guide the exploration of the search space efficiently. This includes techniques like Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS), which balances exploration and exploitation to find high-performing pipelines.

## 5: Termination Criteria
The search process terminates based on predefined criteria, such as a timeout or a satisfactory performance threshold being reached. Once terminated, the best pipeline found during the search is selected for further use or deployment.

## 6: Logging and Reporting
Throughout the process, detailed logs are maintained, recording the construction and evaluation of pipelines. These logs provide insights into the search process, including the pipelines explored, their performance scores, and the final best pipeline selected.

## Step 7: Final Pipeline Selection
The final step involves selecting the best-performing pipeline based on the evaluation scores. This pipeline is then considered for deployment in the target application or further refinement.

## Conclusion
The AutoML process involves a systematic and iterative approach to exploring the space of possible pipelines, guided by heuristics and evaluation metrics. The process aims to efficiently discover high-performing pipelines that meet the specified criteria, leveraging techniques such as MCTS to balance exploration and exploitation.
