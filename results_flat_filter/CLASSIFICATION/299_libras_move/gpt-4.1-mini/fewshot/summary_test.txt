### Termination
- The search process stopped due to a timeout signal (signal 15).

**Summary**:  
The AutoML system profiled a dataset of 90 fully numeric float features (mostly geospatial coordinates). With no missing data detected, it conducted a Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS) to synthesize and explore multiple pipelines by successively applying preprocessing (scaling and feature selection) and diverse classifiers. Multiple simulations refined pipeline configurations, achieving incremental improvements in evaluation scores. The search terminated after exploring a variety of pipelines within the allowed time, discovering 30 candidate pipelines combining different scalers, feature selectors, and classifiers.
